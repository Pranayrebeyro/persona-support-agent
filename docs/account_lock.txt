Account Lock Troubleshooting

Accounts may be locked due to multiple failed login attempts or suspicious activity.

Possible Causes:

* Incorrect password entered repeatedly.
* Security policies triggered.
* Unusual login location detected.

Resolution Steps:

1. Wait 15 minutes before retrying.
2. Reset your password.
3. Verify your email address.
4. Contact support if the issue persists.

Locked accounts are automatically unlocked after the cooldown period unless flagged for security review.
