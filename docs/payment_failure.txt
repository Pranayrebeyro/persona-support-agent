Payment Failure Troubleshooting

Payment transactions may fail due to various reasons.

Common Causes:

* Insufficient account balance.
* Expired debit or credit card.
* Incorrect payment information.
* Bank restrictions.

Recommended Actions:

1. Verify card details.
2. Ensure sufficient balance.
3. Retry after 10 minutes.
4. Contact your bank if necessary.

Supported Payment Methods:

* Credit Card
* Debit Card
* UPI
