Email Verification Guide

Email verification ensures account security and enables account recovery.

Common Issues:

* Verification email not received.
* Email sent to spam folder.
* Expired verification link.

Resolution Steps:

1. Check spam or junk folders.
2. Verify the registered email address.
3. Request a new verification email.
4. Wait up to 5 minutes before retrying.

Verification links expire after 24 hours.
